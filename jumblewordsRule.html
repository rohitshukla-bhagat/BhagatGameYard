B JUMBLE WORDS

📜 Game Summary 📚

➡️ Welcome to Jumble Words, a fun game where you see jumbled letters and guess the word formed based on them! With 50 exciting levels :

  • Easy Levels: 1 to 25
  • Medium Levels: 26 to 40
  • Hard Levels: 41 to 50

➡️ You have 5 chances for each round. If you guess correctly, your score increases by 1; if you fail all 5 chances, you lose the round, and your score decreases by 1. After completing all 50 levels, you'll receive a congratulations message with your score! 🎊

🧩 Jumble Words Game Rules 🎮

1. 🕵️‍♂️ Guess the Word: Arrange the jumbled letters shown to guess the word.

2. 💬 Input Your Word: Type your guess into the input box.

3. ✅ Check Your Answer: Click the Match button to see if your word is correct.

4. 📣 Result Message: A message will display whether your guess is right or wrong.

5. 🎉 Correct Guess: If you're right, your score increases by 1! Click the Next Level button and repeat steps 1 - 4 .

6. ❌ Incorrect Guess: If wrong, you have 1 more chance to input your word. If your guess is wrong again…

7. 🔍 Hint Time: Click the Hint button to see a hint in the input box's placeholder.

8. 🔄 Extra Chances: You get 3 more chances after using the hint. If you can't guess the word within 5 chances, you lose that round, and your score decreases by 1.

9. 🔄 Restart the Game: Click the Restart button to start over from Level 1.

10. 🌟 Level Categories:

  • Easy Levels: 1 to 25
  • Medium Levels: 26 to 40
  • Hard Levels: 41 to 50
