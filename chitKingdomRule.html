B CHIT KINGDOM

📜 Game Summary 🎉

➡️ Chit Kingdom is inspired by the traditional Indian children's game known as Wazir Badshah. In this game, 4 players compete using 4 cards: King, Prince, Soldier, and Thief.

➡️ Scoring:

  • King: 5000 points
  • Prince: 1000 points
  • Soldier: 500 points
  • Thief: 0 points

➡️ The player holding the King card is safe in each round and reveals their card. The player with the Prince card selects the Thief from the other players (excluding the King). If the Prince guesses correctly, points are awarded based on the card. If the guess is incorrect, the Prince's score drops to 0, and the Thief gains 1000 points

➡️ Players continue this process through multiple rounds, culminating in the final round where they can view their scores. Enjoy the game! 🎊

🎭 Chit Kingdom Game Rules 👑

1. 📝 Enter Player Names: Input the names of the players and the number of chances.

2. 🚫 Name Restrictions: Player names must not contain numbers or special characters.

3. ⚖️ Valid Chances: The number of chances should be greater than 0.

4. 🎮 Start the Game: Click the Play button to begin.

5. 🔄 Rotate Cards: Click the Jumble button to shuffle the cards.

6. 👑 Prince's Turn: The player holding the Prince card has the right to select the Thief among the hidden cards.

7. ✅ Check Selection: Click the Check button to reveal the choice.

8. 📊 Update Scores: Scores will be added in the scores section.

9. ➡️ Next Round: Click the Next Round button to continue.

10. 🔄 Repeat: Repeat steps 5-9 for each round. In the final round, click the Final Result button to check player scores after all rounds are completed.
